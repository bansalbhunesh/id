## Design Context

### Users
IDBI Innovate judges, bank executives, credit underwriters, and product/technology reviewers evaluating a Stage-1 PoC for MSME financial health scoring. They need to see a credible underwriting decision quickly, inspect why the decision is defensible, and understand how the prototype maps to bank governance and Stage-2 sandbox integration.

### Brand Personality
Sober, decisive, regulator-ready. The interface should feel like a bank-grade credit cockpit rather than a startup landing page: dense enough for repeated review, clear enough for a live three-minute demo, and confident without hype.

### Aesthetic Direction
Elegant decision stage over an Indian MSME credit register: a warm passbook-paper work surface, faint GST/passbook rules, a top review index, a horizontal borrower file strip, one stamped approval card, and translucent half-page slide-over packets for evidence, governance, proof, and sources. Avoid generic fintech gradients, green/red success semantics, oversized marketing heroes, badge carpets, and card dumps.

### Palette And Type
- Ledger Ink `#1B2A2F`: primary document text and register structure.
- Passbook Paper `#F4EBDD`: main background and sheet warmth.
- GST Ruled Blue `#2E5E7E`: table rules, source headings, and financial figures.
- Stamp Violet `#5B4A8B`: assessment stamp, selected tabs, and risk mark.
- Marigold Audit `#C88A2D`: audit notes and truth-boundary callouts.
- Entry Red `#9B3A32`: decline/correction entries only.
- Display: Libre Baskerville. Body: Source Sans 3. Utility: Azeret Mono.

### Design Principles
- Lead with one rejected-to-approved NTC decision reversal and keep the first viewport calm enough that the judge knows where to look.
- Show governance as product surface, not appendix: explainability, audit, model limits, and fairness controls must be visible.
- Prefer compact banker workflows over marketing copy; every visible element should support a judge demo or credit review.
- Use a top-index plus decision-stage shell, sentence-case headings, one risk mark only, slide-over review packets, and tabular proof panels before adding any new decorative treatment.
- Keep the app deployable as a static frontend served by FastAPI; no build tooling. External web fonts are allowed with system fallbacks.
